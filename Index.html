⚔️ Privacy Policy - Sword Serpents

Last Updated: January 2025

Adam Ferguson ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Sword Serpents ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you play our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Name: You can enter a name (up to 10 characters) for leaderboard display
  • Game Progress: Your scores, levels completed, and gameplay statistics

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Gameplay Data: Game sessions, level progress, scores, and in-game actions
  • Network Information: Internet connection status to enable online features
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ad views and interactions (collected by AdMob)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google AdMob: Collects data for advertising purposes (see Section 4)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To provide core game features, save progress, and maintain leaderboards
  • Leaderboard: To display player names and scores on the global leaderboard
  • Personalization: To enhance your gaming experience and remember your preferences
  • Analytics: To understand how players interact with the game and improve gameplay
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. This data remains private unless you choose to submit scores to the leaderboard.

3.2 Server Storage

Leaderboard data (player names and scores) is stored on secure MongoDB servers with industry-standard encryption.

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all network communications
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and process data including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Location data (approximate)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information

4.2 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

5. Children's Privacy

Sword Serpents is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect sensitive personal information
  • Uses age-appropriate ad settings through AdMob
  • Does not contain social features or chat functionality

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Deletion

  • Local Data: You can clear local game data by uninstalling the App
  • Leaderboard Data: Contact us to request removal of your leaderboard entry

6.2 Consent Withdrawal

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App from your device.

6.3 Network Access

You can play the game offline. However, leaderboard features and ad-based rewards require an internet connection.

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear data
  • Leaderboard Data: Retained indefinitely to maintain leaderboard integrity
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 24 months

8. International Users

Sword Serpents is available globally. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updates to the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
